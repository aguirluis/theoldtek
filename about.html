About Me

I'm Luis E. Aguirre, also known as TheOldTek — a Cloud System Administrator, cybersecurity enthusiast, and home lab architect passionate about building reliable, scalable, and secure systems.

My journey started with tinkering on old hardware and evolved into designing full enterprise-style labs using Proxmox, pfSense, TrueNAS, Azure, AWS, and identity management systems like Samba AD DS and Windows Server.
