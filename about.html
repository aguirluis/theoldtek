About Me

I’m Luis E. Aguirre — better known as TheOldTek — a Cloud System Administrator, cybersecurity enthusiast, and home lab architect who loves building systems that are reliable, scalable, and secure.

You’ve landed at theoldtek, the corner of the internet where a slightly seasoned IT guy still keeps pace with the new kids. My tech journey kicked off after the military, followed by earning my associate degree in 2007 and my bachelor’s in 2009. Since then, I’ve spent more than a decade wrangling servers, taming identity systems, and convincing cloud platforms to cooperate. I work alongside plenty of younger admins and developers these days, which is exactly why the name fits. I might be “the old tech,” but I’m still learning, still building, and still having a blast doing it.

What started with tinkering on old hardware has grown into designing full enterprise‑style labs and environments using Proxmox, pfSense, TrueNAS, Azure, AWS, and identity management platforms like Samba AD DS and Windows Server. Whether it’s automation, virtualization, or security hardening, I enjoy turning complex systems into clean, dependable solutions.
