About Me

I’m Luis E. Aguirre — better known as TheOldTek — an Associate Cloud & Systems Administrator with a passion for building reliable, scalable, and secure infrastructure. I work across both on‑prem and cloud environments, blending years of hands‑on systems administration with a growing focus on Azure and AWS.

My tech journey started after the military, followed by earning my associate degree in 2007 and my bachelor’s in 2009. Over the past decade, I’ve supported identity systems, managed servers, troubleshot complex infrastructure issues, and built out home labs that mirror real enterprise environments. The name “TheOldTek” fits — I’ve been around long enough to remember how things used to be, but I’m still learning, still experimenting, and still having fun keeping up with the new wave of cloud‑driven tech.

What began with tinkering on old hardware has grown into designing full lab architectures using Proxmox, pfSense, TrueNAS, Azure, AWS, and identity platforms like Samba AD DS and Windows Server. I enjoy taking complex systems — whether virtualized, cloud‑based, or hybrid — and turning them into clean, dependable solutions.
